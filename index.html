Bachelor of Early Education (BECEd)

VISION

We envision being a leader in early childhood education, recognized for producing excellent teachers who ensure high-quality early learning for all children.

MISSION

Our mission is to prepare skilled, passionate educators who foster the holistic development of young children through innovative, inclusive, and culturally responsive teaching practices.

OBJECTIVE

Our objectives include developing a deep understanding of child development and best practices, enhancing practical teaching and inclusive education techniques, preparing for diverse educational settings, promoting continuous professional development, empowering students to advocate for children's rights, and fostering partnerships with families and communities.

Bachelor of Science in Agribusiness (BSAB)

VISION

We aim to be a top agribusiness program, producing leaders who drive sustainable practices and innovative business solutions in the agricultural sector.

MISSION

The mission of our program is to develop innovative, ethical agribusiness professionals who can manage agricultural enterprises sustainably and address global agribusiness challenges.

OBJECTIVE

Our objectives involve blending business management with agricultural science, developing market analysis and financial skills, educating on sustainable agricultural practices, encouraging innovation and entrepreneurship, cultivating leadership and decision-making skills, preparing for the global agribusiness environment, and promoting industry and community partnerships.

Bachelor of Science in Criminology (BSCrim)

VISION

The School of Criminology envisions to produce globally competitive graduates with honorable VIRTUES, who have passion for EXCELLENCE, and are devoted to public SERVICE.

MISSION

The mission of the School of Criminology is to provide students with an opportunity to relate theoretical knowledge in Criminology to practical application in order to make them better prepared for life and in their future integration to the work environment as well as to public service.

OBJECTIVE

The objective of the school of criminology is to provide the community with graduates who are professionally competent, morally and socially responsible individuals who can deliver efficient and effective services in the various areas of Criminology.

About Us

COLEGIO DE LAUREL is established by virtue of Municipal Ordinance No. 9, s. 2023 enacted by the Sangguniang Bayan of the Municipality of Laurel, Batangas.

The Colegio de Laurel has a vision to be a center of nation builders that will produce competent, God-loving, and transformative leaders and movers of the society and empowered communities.

This is a locally funded higher educational institution (HEI) which will offer the following initial academic programs: Bachelor of Early Childhood Education (BECEd), Bachelor of Science in Agribusiness (BSAB) and Bachelor of Science in Criminology (BSCrim).
